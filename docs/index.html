Simple by design

⚙️

Lives in the menu bar

No window, no dock icon. Just a discreet percentage in your menu bar that's always visible.

⚡

Near-zero cost

Each refresh uses a single Haiku token (~$0.00001). Check your limits without burning your budget.

📦

Zero dependencies

Pure Swift + SwiftUI. No Electron, no Node, no frameworks. Under 400 lines of code.

🔐

Your key stays local

API key is stored locally on your Mac. No server, no telemetry, no data collection.

🎨

Color-coded bars

Green, orange, red — see your remaining capacity at a glance without reading numbers.

🕐

Reset countdown

Live countdown shows exactly when your rate limits reset. No more guessing.

Up and running in 30 seconds
